# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Given that the Moral Spectrometry Engine deals with ethical reasoning, we expect contributors to model the behavior we're trying to evaluate.

## Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** in all interactions
- **Assume good faith** when disagreements arise
- **Focus on substance**, not personal attacks
- **Acknowledge uncertainty** in moral questions
- **Credit others' work** appropriately
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be constructive in feedback and criticism

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Deliberate intimidation or trolling
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public space where an individual represents the project.

## Enforcement

Violations will be addressed by maintainers through:

1. **Warning** — For first-time or minor violations
2. **Temporary ban** — For repeated or moderate violations
3. **Permanent ban** — For severe or persistent violations

## Reporting

Report violations to **opensource@godson.ai**. All reports will be reviewed confidentially.
